Dress Code

Dress code policy is enforced at ALL of our tournaments and clinics.
Parents must also follow dress code at Salinas Golf & CC and Corral de Tierra CC.
We appreciate that these two private clubs allow us to use their courses. Please help us to thank them by showing respect for their rules.
  • Soft spikes or tennis shoes
  • Collared shirts tucked in (no tank tops)
  • Slacks or golf shorts (no blue jeans, sweat pants, or short shorts)

Spectator Info

Reminder to all Spectators:
It is wonderful when spectators support our junior golfers. In order to be fair to everyone, please keep the following rules in mind:
  1. You are a spectator only. Your child must carry his own clubs or pull his own cart.
  2. You must refrain from talking to, socializing with or giving advice to any junior player.
  3. You must refrain from talking to, socializing with or giving advice to any junior player.
  4. You must stay 25 yards from the competitors, using cart paths or staying to the sides of fairway. You are never to be on the tee blocks or the putting greens.
  5. You must not get involved in any rules decisions during competitive play. All decisions must be that of the junior.
  6. Following play, all rules decisions will be made by the attending golf pro or the Executive Director.
  7. If you see a rules infraction or a breach of etiquette, please bring it to the attention of the Executive Director.
  8. If your child has a scorekeeper, let him handle all rulings.
